Fase 2: BlackScholes y pricing binario

Terico:

  • Reescribe las frmulas: $$ d_1 = \frac{\ln(S/K)+(r+\tfrac12\sigma^2)\tau}{\sigma\sqrt\tau}, \quad d_2 = d_1 - \sigma\sqrt\tau. $$

  • European Financial Option:

    $C = S\,N(d_1)-K\,e^{-r\tau}N(d_2)$

    $P = K\,e^{-r\tau}N(-d_2)-S\,N(-d_1)$

  • European Binary Options: Cashornothing y Assetornothing:

    $C^{CN}=K\,e^{-r\tau}N(d_2)$

    $P^{CN}=K\,e^{-r\tau}N(-d_2)$

    $C^{AN}=S\,N(d_1)$

    $P^{AN}=S\,N(-d_1)$
